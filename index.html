Time 2 Trade: Trading Clock + Economic Events

Time 2 Trade keeps futures and forex day traders aligned with session overlaps and scheduled catalysts using a New York time-first clock plus an economic events workspace.

Key features

  • Dual-circle session clock with overlaps and active session detection
  • Economic events timeline/table with impact and currency filters (workspace unlocks with a free account)
  • Favorites and personal notes synced for authenticated users
  • Exports (CSV/JSON) from the events workspace
  • PWA install for fast launch and a New York time-first workflow

Built for active traders

Designed for futures and forex day traders who need session awareness, overlap timing, and real-time economic event context without heavy dashboards or trading signals.
