About Time 2 Trade

Time 2 Trade is a visual trading intelligence platform designed for futures and forex day traders who need to stay aligned with global market sessions. Our dual-circle session clock makes it easy to see when markets are active, overlay high-impact economic events, and plan your trading day with confidence.

What makes Time 2 Trade different

Unlike simple world clocks, Time 2 Trade gives you a complete picture of the trading day in one glance. AM hours sit on the inner ring, PM hours on the outer ring, overlaid with live economic events and timezone-aware insights.

Whether you track London open, New York session, or Asian market hours, the clock adapts to your timezone automatically so you always know when each session starts, ends, and how much time remains.

Key features

  • Visual session intelligence: Eight customizable sessions on dual rings with active-session highlights.
  • Live economic events overlay: High-impact events plotted directly on the session clock.
  • Timezone intelligence: Automatic conversion to your local timezone.
  • Cloud sync settings: Save preferences across devices with a free account.
  • Customizable sessions: Rename, recolor, and resize sessions to match your playbook.
  • Session alerts: Visual indicators show current and upcoming sessions.
  • Responsive design: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

How to use Time 2 Trade

Getting started: Visit time2.trade and the platform immediately displays session intelligence in your timezone.

Customize: Adjust session times, colors, and labels. Filter economic events by impact and currency pairs.

View events: Toggle the events overlay to see when major news releases occur and click through for details.

Change timezones: Switch timezones to plan travel or coordinate with other desks.

Save your setup: Create a free account to sync your configuration across devices.

Who uses Time 2 Trade

Built for active day traders working with futures and forex pairs who rely on session timing (ICT killzones, London/New York opens) and economic catalysts.

Technology and data

Powered by React, Vite, and Material Design components for a fast, responsive experience. Economic events come from the JBlanked News Calendar API, the same professional MQL5 source used by institutional platforms.

Free to use

Time 2 Trade is free with all intelligence features available to every account. We built it for our own trading, and we keep improving it with community feedback.
